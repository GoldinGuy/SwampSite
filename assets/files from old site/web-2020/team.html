Who We Are:

Team 179 is primarily made up of students from Suncoast Community High School and Inlet Groves Community High School; however, we have several students from other high schools throughout Palm Beach County.

Our mentors include engineers from Belcan, Lockheed Martin, EDF, Pratt Whitney and retirees

Team Impact:

Team 179 allows students to apply learned knowledge to real life situations while working side-by-side professional engineers.

To promote FIRST Robotics and educate the community about robots, we have done several demonstrations including but not limited to: Palm Beach County School Board, South Florida Science Museum, Stuart Air Show, Regal Cinema, and 4-H. Build Season
