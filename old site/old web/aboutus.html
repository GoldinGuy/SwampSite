We, Team 179, are united as the proud creators of the "swamp Thing". Each year we create a new design that meets the changing standards and requirements set before us by FIRST. Our team of students, teachers, and engineers work together to identify and implement innovative solutions to the FIRST yearly challenge.

    We must design, build, and test our robot in a mere six weeks. We have focused our efforts on meeting the game challenge, meeting FIRST's expectations, and earning the pride of our sponsors, our parents, our teachers, and the schools we represent.
